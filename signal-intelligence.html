⚡ Telegram Setup Required
1
Open Telegram and search for @BotFather — send it /newbot and follow the prompts. Copy the bot token it gives you.
2
Send any message to your new bot. Then open this URL in your browser, replacing TOKEN: https://api.telegram.org/botTOKEN/getUpdates — find your chat_id in the response.
3
Paste both values in the Telegram Config section below and click Save. Then hit Test to confirm alerts are working.
4
Set your alert threshold (how many green signals before Telegram fires) and enable Auto-scan. That's it.
